Who is JUSTIN Haney to you?
Well to put it simple.

I am a Full Stack Developer with a passion for coding and growing. While going to school for computer network and administration, I developed a taste for coding with my music on. This was the first time I truly got lost in my work and enjoyed it. Starting my Career at a company taking incoming calls for a company product. I wanted more with my career and my life. I knew that skill would never transfer. So I did what so many have done before me, and will continue to do afterwards.

I taught myself web development. I transfer to my companies web development. I continued to push myself to learn more and grow. Starting with HTML and CSS all the way to Javascript. Still going strong til this day. Working with powerful tools I am learning daily. Javascript skills have pushed me to learn more from the DOM Control to React. Working with Node.js to create web apps for my team and company on many different levels.

So simply, a man with a passion, a dream, and a desire to code.
